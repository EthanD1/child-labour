Child labour can be stopped



Child labour is described by Unicef as work undertaken by a child that is harmful to them in some way. The harm could be to their health, safety, or ability to have a childhood. Child labour inteferes with children's education and deprives them of the right to normal physical and mental development.

How we can help

You can help by buying products made by companies that don't use child labour in their products. If you search up the company name followed by child labour then you should get some results.
